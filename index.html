Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tariff Totaler

Is Tariff Totaler completely free?

Yes, Tariff Totaler is 100% free to use. There are no subscriptions, no premium tiers, and no hidden fees. We believe consumers have the right to know the full impact of trade policies on their purchases.

Does this work on websites other than Amazon?

Currently, Tariff Totaler only works on Amazon.com. We've specialized in detecting Amazon's specific layout to provide you with the most accurate tariff information. We may expand to other retailers in the future.

How accurate are the tariff calculations?

Our calculations are based on the official tariff schedules from recent trade policy changes. However, they are for informational purposes only. The exact tariff applied may vary based on specific product categories, exemptions, and how retailers choose to distribute these costs.

Why did prices suddenly go up on so many products?

Recent changes in trade policies have introduced new or increased tariffs on goods from various countries. These additional costs are often passed on to consumers, resulting in higher retail prices. Tariff Totaler helps you see exactly how much of that price increase is due to these policy changes.

Does this extension collect my data?

No. Tariff Totaler works entirely in your browser and doesn't collect or transmit any of your personal data or browsing habits. We value your privacy and believe in transparent tools that respect user data.

Are these tariffs permanent?

Trade policies can change with different administrations and economic strategies. Tariff Totaler updates automatically to reflect current policies as they evolve. What's certain is that we'll always keep you informed about how these policies affect your wallet.
